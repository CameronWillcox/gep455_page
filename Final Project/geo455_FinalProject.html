Injured Bald Eagles

Large wild birds are often injured and killed due to human causes. Bald Eagles make up a large pecentage of injured wildlife in Wisconsin. Wildlife rehabilitation centers present a helpful resource for the assistance of sick, injured, or orphaned wild animals. It is important to know that if you find injured wildlife, not all hope is lost. Each year, many injured animals are brought into wildlife rehabilitaton centers and are nurtured back to health. This map show the locations of of injury for numerous injured Bald Eagles. Click around to learn more about what kind of injury each Eagle had and see what rehabilitation center it was brought to receive treatment.
