Injured Large Wild Birds in Wisconsin

Injured Bald Eagles

Large wild birds are often injured and killed due to human causes. Bald Eagles make up a large pecentage of injured wildlife in Wisconsin.

Wildlife rehabilitation centers present a helpful resource for the assistance of sick, injured, or orphaned wild animals.

Map Information

This map shows four wildlife rehabilitation centers around the Wisconsisn/ Minnesota area. Each point and line shows an injured bald eagle and what rehabilitation center it was taken too and treated at.
