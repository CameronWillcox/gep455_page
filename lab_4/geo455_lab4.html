Lab 4: Seven Wonders and Points of Interest

     

About this assignment

In this assignment, we are working with layer controls. We have three layers, landmarks, basemap, and SRTM 30m hillshade map using WMS service. Landmarks are based on encyclopedia Britannica's entry on New Seven Wonders of the World and the images are linked from Wikipedia Common images. I have added a few extra points of my own to show places that I would like to vsit around the world
